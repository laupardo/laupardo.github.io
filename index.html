About me

Ever since I was little I've had a thing for food. During the weekends I would wake up and head straight to the kitchen to cook eggs when I was barely tall enough to reach the stove on a stool. I would watch the food network and cooking shows when others were busy watching cartoons. Now that I've grown up, life has led me through many paths yet my love of food seems to be a constant. I'm currently a Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science and Engineering major at University of the Andes who, when she isn't coding for Web Development, (yeah, it sometimes exists) likes to go to restaurants and develop recipes inspired by cultures, people and celebrations! Outside of the realm of food I love sports. I used to be a roller hockey player but now I like to stick to Tennis, the gym and Swimming. I'm currently enrolled in 7 courses at my univeristy Web Development, Robotics, Business analytics, Enterprise Systems, Network Infrastructure, German and Afro-Colombian Music.

Bon apetit!
